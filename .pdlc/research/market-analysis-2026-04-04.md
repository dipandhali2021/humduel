## Consumer Behavior Trends

- Users spend <6% of smartphone time in browsers (native apps dominate)
- BUT web has structural advantages for: viral discovery, cross-device use, institutional accessibility
- Interactive content: 2-3x higher conversions, 85% completion rate
- Apps with social sharing: 35% higher retention, 20% higher engagement
- Gen Z/Alpha: no psychological distinction between "app" and "website"; prioritize shareability and identity expression

## Monetization Models

| Model | Conversion | Revenue/User | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hard paywall | ~10.7% day-35 | $3-10+ | High-value daily utility |
| Freemium + subscription | 2-5% | $0.10-$1.50 | Social/daily habit apps |
| Ad-supported | N/A (100%) | $0.01-$0.10 | High-volume casual games |
| Hybrid (ads + upgrade) | Variable | $0.08 ARPDAU | Broad entertainment |

Key finding: hybrid monetization outperforms pure models. Hard paywalls convert 5x better but freemium drives network effects.
